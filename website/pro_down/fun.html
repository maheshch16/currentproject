FunJoy
Description:

Funjoy is smartcard based product suite ideal for enhancing customer experience in hospitality and entertainment. It creates a secure, convenient and memorable stay for guests while also promoting the brand and providing higher efficiencies in administrative control to the management.

Key Features :

  • Smartcard based cashless payments at restaurants, gaming zones, food courts,etc.
  • Shorter waiting lines and faster movements with service management.
  • Automated access and restriction across the entertainment zone.
  • Tracking and monitoring of both personnel, guests and visitors assets withing the resort premises.
  • Touch-screen based ticket vendling, card topup, information kiosk and facility usage.
  • Mobile hand-held units for recreational programs and auto-guide for guests.
  • Centralized command and control to monitor and housekeeping, garden workers and others.
  • Loyalty management with coupons and offer along with promotion designing.
  • Usage and facility based pricing management.
  • Hands-free fun with smart-band.
